Desktop Publishing

The final step in a translation project is often desktop publishing, or the adaptation of digital files for multiple languages. This is when we take all your translated content and ensure there is seamless visual communication by preserving or enhancing the document design and layout. We guarantee that all files print properly, making sure communications get out in a timely manner.

In-House Design Team

Why consider
desktop publishing?

After a document has gone through translation and editing, it’s important that the final piece also look as professional as you have worked to make it sound. Our desktop publishing services ensure that your printed material, websites, or digital media look just as great (if not better) than it did in its original form.
