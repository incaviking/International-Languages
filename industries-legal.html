Legal

Since our inception, we have offered legal/judicial interpreters and translation services in many languages. We have extensive knowledge of the United States legal system, following a strict code of ethics and consortium guidelines.

Provided Services

Court Interpreters

Our experienced interpreters work with intake, family commissioner, civil, children’s, traffic and criminal courts. We can also be available for District Attorney offices to interpret for victims and witnesses.

Document Translation

Legal documents and notices can be translated by our certified judicial professionals. We can also transcribe and translate recordings for district attorney’s offices and law enforcement.

Depositions

Certified and qualified interpreters or teams of interpreters can interpret for legal depositions under oath at attorney’s offices. The right professional interpreter makes a deposition flow smoothly.

Attorney Jail Visits/PSR's

Our interpreters accompany defense attorneys who are visiting their clients in jail and prisons. We can also interpret for state officers, attending interviews at homes or detention centers to prepare a Pre-Sentence Report (PSR).

Probation and Parole

State probation and parole officers may need an interpreter to bridge the language gap during community supervision for the Department of Corrections. You can count on our interpreters to communicate effectively.

Accurate, Clear and To-The-Point

Our interpreters specialize in legal matters, as well as language to make sure your legal interpretations and translations are accurate, clear and to-the-point. Our legal language services are only a click away. Get a free quote today.

IMPARTIALITY, PROFESSIONALISM, AND ACCREDITATION

Simultaneous Interpreting

Most legal interpreting is completed using the simultaneous chuchotage (whispering mode) or headset equipment, especially for jury trials. Judicial interpreters must have knowledge of the legal system and the mental agility to make sense of it into another language.

Certified Interpreters

The use of competent state or federal court interpreters in proceedings involving speakers of languages other than English is critical to ensure that justice is carried out fairly. Our certified interpreters make sense of the formal and technical jargon of legal proceedings in a way that the defendants can understand.
