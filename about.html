About

ABOUT US

Passion for languages.
compassion for people.

We got started in the language business back in 1995. We began as a medical interpreter agency, working with local clients in the Green Bay, Wisconsin area to communicate crucial health information. We soon branched out into government work, and we began to expand our reach. Now, we are a full-service language agency with over 500 partner organizations across the country.

Our goal is always to help our clients communicate as effectively and easily as possible. We pride ourselves on our comprehensive array of services performed by the best linguists in the industry. We believe that everyone in the world should be able to communicate with one another, and we are proud that we are able to enable that for Limited English Proficient (LEP) people and companies.

Always Learning. Always Improving.

Training & Workshops

We believe language learning is a lifelong process. Our trainers have more than 40 years of combined experienced teaching interpretation skills to students. They cover topics such as linguistic proficiency, ethics, interpreting modes, interpreter dilemmas, the roles of the interpreter, regulatory compliance, and more. Senior interpreters tutor others with interactive teachings. Training and workshops are continuously updated to ensure everyone knows the latest information about the profession.

Linguistic Testing & Evaluations

We stress the importance of certification and recertification for national interpreter programs. Our company has qualified interpreter professionals within our network. New employees and members are rigorously vetted to ensure our customers receive only the highest quality services. Our language evaluations are industry-specific, covering industries such as medical, judicial, government, educational, manufacturing, insurance and more.

Compliance Program

One of the ways we maintain our commitment to providing quality services is by adhering to compliance with industry regulations and client policies. Our compliance department works diligently to make sure we are up-to-date on all policies and that our programs are compliant with the industries we work in. We ensure that everyone on our staff receives this kind of training on a consistent basis.
