About

Fluent

INTERPRETER SCHEDULING

scheduling, billing and analytics on one hipaa compliant interpreter network.

Fluent is a game-changing web and mobile software application that brings interpreter services into the modern age. You can use it to track your interpreter requests from beginning to end, all from one centralized and easy-to-use application.

SCHEDULING INTERPRETING

Scheduling Made Easy

Stop playing phone tag with your scheduling requests. Simply log in to Fluent and schedule interpreters. Our team of schedulers and interpreters work together to make things flow seamlessly.

INVOICE & BILLING

Paper-free, Hassle-free Invoicing

The data we collect on each Interpreting assignment allows us to streamline billing and provides more detailed and transparent Invoices for your review.

DATA & ANALYTICS

Analytics With More Insights

Fluent uses real time data to provide better insights about how interpreters and your language service providers are performing in real time. You can see all your historical usage of language services and generate reports to measure service-level compliance.

JOIN OUR NETWORK

Be a part of a growing community of skilled linguists as a translator or interpreter with International Languages. We are always on the lookout for top talent to add to a growing roster of passionate people who want to help the world communicate better.
