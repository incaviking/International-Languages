Why is proofreading so important in translation?

Translation proofreading is the crucial final stage of a translation process. Proofreaders painstakingly comb through the contents to spot any errors. With many parts of the translation process being automated these days, proofreading is one area where human intervention is required. Machine translations are fast but not fool-proof.

Elements of Proofreading

Grammar Punctuation Spelling Formatting Typos Essence of Content as Per the Audience’s Culture, Language and Demographics Visual Consistency Abbreviations and Expansions Testing Broken Website Links Structuring of Sentences Length of Text Identifying Inappropriate Words and Phrases Capitalization and Usage of Tenses
